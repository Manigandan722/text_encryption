What is Cryptography?

Cryptography is the practice of securing information by converting it into a coded format that can only be accessed or understood by authorized parties.

cryptography typically involves the use of encryption to transform plaintext (unencrypted) data into ciphertext (encrypted) data, and decryption to reverse the process and convert ciphertext back into plaintext.
